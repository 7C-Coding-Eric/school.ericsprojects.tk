Organized Governments

The yellow river’s government structure was Social and Political, meaning that it was an aristocracy run by kings and upper class citizens. The Yellow River was ruled from Xi’an.

Complex Religions

The two main religions of the Yellow River Valley Civilization were Confucianism and Daoism.

Job Specialization

The three main jobs people living around the Yellow River were Farming, Merchants, and Military. Farming provided food for all the people, Merchants sold goods such as clothes and the tools necessary to survive, and the military protected the citizens.

Social Classes

The Social Classes in order: king and his relatives, nobles, craftspeople, traders, farmers, and lastly slaves.

Writing

The Shang people etched characters (pictures) onto bones. Their writing is also known as 'oracle bone script'. Other ancient scripts, such as Egypt's hieroglyphics, fell out of use, but the oracle bone script developed into the modern characters, which Chinese people still use today.

Public Works

The Chinese people mastered primitive irrigation techniques, which contributed to the formation of the basic farmland irrigation system capable of water storage, channeling, irrigating and draining in the west. All of this took hard human, manual labor but benefited the society and helped the Shang dynasty to thrive during their reign.
